About SEDS Trinity

Space Exploration Outreach from Nepal

Our Mission

SEDS Trinity is a student-led organization dedicated to promoting space science education and exploration in Nepal. We conduct outreach programs, workshops, and hands-on projects to inspire young minds to pursue careers in aerospace and related fields.

Meet Our President

IYPT 2026 Qualifier from Nepal

Our president has represented Nepal at the International Young Physicists Tournament (IYPT), showcasing exceptional talent in physics research and problem-solving.

Under their leadership, SEDS Trinity continues to expand its outreach programs and inspire students across the country.

What is SEDS?

Students for the Exploration and Development of Space (SEDS) is an international student organization dedicated to promoting space exploration. SEDS Trinity is proud to be part of this global network, bringing space education to Nepal.
